Coach's Guide

🚀 Analysis

Select a **Sample Game** or use **Upload** to process your own tracking data. Click **Run Analysis** to generate events.

📍 Event Map

View detected passes (arrows) and shots (red dots) on the 2D pitch. Hover events for details.

🎬 Match Replay

Visual animation of player movement. Load frames to watch the tactical positioning in real-time.

⚽ Build-Up Analysis

Find the **Key Moments** section at the bottom. Click **Watch Build-Up** to automatically jump the replay to the exact passing sequence that led to a goal.
